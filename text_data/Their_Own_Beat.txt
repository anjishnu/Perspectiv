
        The joy of fashion high jinks, the witty hat, the spontaneous mix of color, the exuberance of pattern, are the language of a few who live in their own world, where wearers become the authors of their own looks. In this age of conformity and big brands, individualism is a delightful rarity. Seen here are champions who dress with the freedom of their imaginations, in the spirit of Bergdorf's holiday windows, above.  ON THE STREET
