
        FENDERSON--Diane Newton December 7, 2006. Graduated Meredith College, NC. Career executive, business owner, gifted artist. Diane loved to travel and most enjoyed time spent living in Italy. Her wit, talent, intelligence and integrity inspired us. Her laughter and compassion comforted us. Beloved daughter of James Edgar and Evelyn Newton, lovingly remembered and survived by her sister Suzanne Newton Sarafian, nephew Steven Sarafian, nieces Stacy Diane Sarafian, Salli Orcutt, several great nieces and nephews, husband and then dear friend Richard Fenderson. A private family service was held. Donations may be made to Pulmonary Rehab Lung Failure Fund, NY Presbyterian Hospital.
