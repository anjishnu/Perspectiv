
        Karen Beth Kulvin, a daughter of Dale M. Kulvin and Dr. Stephen Kulvin of Coral Gables, Fla., was married last evening to Dr. William Marshall Portnoy, a son of Sonia Portnoy and Dr. Arnold Portnoy of Boynton Beach, Fla. Rabbi Eric Polokoff officiated at the Tribeca Rooftop in New York.
        The bride, 36, will continue to use her name professionally. She is a senior vice president specializing in commercial real estate securities at Dividend Capital Total Realty Trust, a real estate investment trust in New York. She graduated from Tufts and received an M.B.A. from Boston University.
        Her father is the chief of the ophthalmology department at Mount Sinai Medical Center in Miami Beach. Her mother is the vice president of Kurland Associates, a real estate company in Miami.
        The bridegroom, 44, is an ear, nose and throat physician specializing in facial plastic and reconstructive surgery in New York. He graduated from Brandeis and received a medical degree from George Washington University. His father, a retired podiatrist, practiced in Bridgeport, Conn.
