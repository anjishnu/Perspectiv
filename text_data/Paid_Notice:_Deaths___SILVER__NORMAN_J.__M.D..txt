
        SILVER--Norman J., M.D. St. Petersburg, Florida. Born February 5th, 1926 in Brooklyn, NY. Son of the late Benjamin Silver and Pauline Nussbaum Silver. Devoted husband of Marcia for 58 years, brother of Richard, father of Nancy, Jacob, and Thomas, grandfather of Eric and Marc Schlossberg, Matthew and Timothy Silver, Catherine and Benjamin Silver. A 1942 graduate of the High School of Music and Art in New York City and Phi Beta Kappa 1948 graduate of Duke University and Medical School. Captain, U.S Army Medical Corps from 1955 to 1957. Retired Orthopedic Surgeon in New Rochelle, NY. President of the New Rochelle Hospital Medical Board from 1983 to 1984, and Chief of Orthopedics. Member of ASPEC (Academy of Senior Professionals of Eckerd College, St. Petersburg, Florida) where he lovingly tended the rose garden. A bicyclist, squashplayer, boater, and traveler, accomplished pianist, oboist, and cellist, he played the cello with various chamber music groups and orchestras. Member of the Tampa Bay Symphony Board. A 12pm funeral home chapel service will be held on Friday at the George T. Davis Funeral Home, 14 Lecount Place, New Rochelle, NY with interment to follow in the Mount Pleasant Cemetery. The family will receive friends at the home of Tom and Diane Silver, 28 Jerome Ave., New Rochelle, NY on Friday from 7pm to 9pm and Saturday from 12pm to 8pm. Contributions to Academy of Senior Professionals at the Eckerd College in honor of Norman J. Silver.
