
        A hit-and-run driver struck and killed a 12-year-old Queens boy yesterday while fleeing the scene of an accident he had been involved in a few blocks away, the police said.
        Jacob Colon was crossing 108th Street at Atlantic Avenue in Ozone Park when he was hit by a speeding black Mercedes-Benz, according to the police. The boy was pronounced dead at Jamaica Hospital Medical Center .
        Officers said the Mercedes had been in an accident on Atlantic Avenue and 112th Street moments before it struck the child.
        Witnesses told the police that the car, driven by a male about 6 feet tall and weighing 200 to 250 pounds, had heavy front end damage.
