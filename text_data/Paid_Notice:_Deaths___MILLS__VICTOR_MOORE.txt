
        MILLS--Victor Moore. Born October 27, 1919, died at home in Roxbury, Connecticut on December 4. After the Navy, he worked for the United Nations from 1946 until 1982. His U.N. career included assignments in Ghana, Israel, Cyprus, and Italy. He attended Swarthmore College and Harvard Business School. He is survived by his wife of 50 years, Inga-Britta Mills, his brother James Mills, his son Karl-Olof Mills, his four daughters Anne Mills, Dr. Evelyn Mills, Catherine Parker and Victoria Mills, and his five grandchildren. He was preceded in death by a son, Christopher Mills. A celebration of his life will be held at Minor Memorial Library in Roxbury at 11:30 am Friday, December 22.
