
        Two men were killed in shootings early yesterday, the police said. In the first shooting, police officers responding to a report of gunfire about 12:40 a.m. found a 20-year-old man shot twice on Strickland Avenue. The man, Cordero McBean of Brooklyn, was pronounced dead at Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center. In a second shooting, Kurt Richard, 22, of East 55th Street, was fatally shot about 4 a.m. at the corner of Nostrand Avenue and Herkimer Place, the police said. No arrests had been made yesterday evening in either shooting.
