
        ALISBERG--Helene. December 11, 2006 in Avon, CT. She was an advocate for the education of young children. She is survived by her daughter Nancy of West Hartford, CT, her son Andrew of Greenwich, CT and four grandchildren. The funeral will be Wednesday, December 13 at 11:00 at Weinstein Mortuary, 640 Farmington Ave., Hartford, CT. The family will sit shiva at her home on Wednesday and Thursday and at the home of her son in Greenwich, CT on Friday.
