
        A 17-month-old girl found unconscious by her baby sitter early yesterday died a short time later, the police said. The girl, whose name was not released, was found in her apartment on Cypress Avenue in Port Morris at 12:05 a.m. yesterday, the police said. She was taken to Lincoln Medical and Mental Health Center and pronounced dead at 12:50 a.m., the police said. A spokeswoman for the medical examiner's office said that an autopsy had not been completed yesterday and that the cause of death remained unknown.
