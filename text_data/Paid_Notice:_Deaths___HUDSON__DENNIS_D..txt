
        HUDSON--Dennis D. Emeritus Professor of World Religions at Smith College, died Sunday at his home in Northampton, MA. He was one of the foremost scholars of the religions of India, noted especially for his interpretation of the Vaikunta Perumal Temple at Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu. He loved cooking, teaching, and enthusiastic discussion an was in turn loved by all. He is survived by his wife Lori Divine, his children Megan, David, Jacob, and Alexa, his granddaughter Alexandra, and his brothers, Bruce, Neal, and Marvin. A memorial service will be held at Smith College in early 2007.
