
        An Islandia man stopped his car and pulled a driver from a burning vehicle on Old Nichols Road early yesterday morning, the Suffolk County police said. The police said the driver, Winston Decoteau, 24, of Brentwood, lost control of his car about 3:30 a.m. and spun into a utility pole. The car caught fire, and a passing driver, James Ivory, 25, pulled over and rescued Mr. Decoteau, the police said. Mr. Decoteau was taken to Stony Brook University Medical Center for treatment of burns, and Mr. Ivory was treated for smoke inhalation, the police said.
