
        Jodie Hollis Grunbaum, the daughter of Barbara and Len Grunbaum of Manalapan, N.J., was married last evening to Michael Kirshenbaum, the son of Donna and Sy Kirshenbaum of Jericho, N.Y. Rabbi Mark Mallach officiated at Temple Beth Ahm in Springfield, N.J.
        Mrs. Kirshenbaum, 32, is a senior print media buyer at Integrated Media Solutions, an advertising company in New York. She graduated from Kent State University.
        Mr. Kirshenbaum, 31, is an assistant vice president in the financial systems support group at the Bank of New York. He graduated cum laude from the State University at Albany and is studying for a master's degree in information security at Pace University in New York.
