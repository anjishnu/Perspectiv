
        HOWERY--Darryl, passed away peacefully in his 70th year on December 6th, 2006. Beloved, respected, husband, father, brother, grandfather and Professor Emeritus of Brooklyn College. Woodrow Wilson fellow, alumnus of Jefferson High School, Roanoke College, University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill and UC Berkeley. Donations may be made in his honor to: Professor Darryl Gilmer Howery Memorial Scholarship (on memo line). Check made out to: Brooklyn College Foundation, 2900 Bedford Ave., Brooklyn, NY 11210.
