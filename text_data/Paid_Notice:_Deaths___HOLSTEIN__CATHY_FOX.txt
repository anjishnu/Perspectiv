
        HOLSTEIN--Cathy Fox, 54, died peacefully at home in Ossining, NY on December 10th, after an eight month fight with lung cancer. Cathy was known internationally in the publishing field for her keen negotiating skills and ability to bring American authors to audiences around the world. Her 26 year career included positions at Random House, the Lois Wallace Literary Agency, Macmillan, and Penguin Putnam. Most recently, Cathy worked at Purls of Yarn in Pleasantville, NY and Hudson Affiliates in Ardsley, NY. Cathy was born in New York City to Jimi and William Fox. She graduated from the Fieldston School and Skidmore College. Cathy lived in Hong Kong and Beijing before returning to New York City and then raising her family in Ardsley, NY. Cathy is survived by her husband of 30 years, business journalist William J. Holstein, and their children, Jason and Alison. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, December 16th at 1pm at the First Unitarian Society of Westchester on Old Jackson Road, in Hastings-on-Hudson, NY. Memorial contributions in Cathy's name may be sent to the Relay for Life, American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, NY 10601.
