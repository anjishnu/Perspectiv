
        HAFT--Clara Halpern, passed away on December 14, 2006 at the age of 96. Beloved wife of 42 years to the late Alex C. Haft, devoted mother to Gail Haft and Herbert (Marilyn) Haft, caring grandmother to Emily (Donald) Bloom, William (Lisa) Haft, and greatgrandmother to Charlie and Tim Bloom, and Rebecca and Alex Haft. Dear aunt to her nieces and nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. She was loved by her family and friends and will be greatly missed. Services to be held Sunday, December 17 at 12:15pm at Temple Beth-El in Great Neck, NY. Interment at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
