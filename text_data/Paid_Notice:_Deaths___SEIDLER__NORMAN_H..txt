
        SEIDLER--Norman H., 87, of Boca Raton, FL, passed away December 17, 2006. Survived by his wife Muriel, daughter and son-in-law Terry and Andy Marcum, daughter and partner Jo Seidler and Bette Skandalis, and granddaughter Lisa Marcum. The three passions in his life were his family, the practice of Antitrust Law, and the NY Giants. Donations may be made to Ovarian Cancer Research Fund. www.ocrf.org
