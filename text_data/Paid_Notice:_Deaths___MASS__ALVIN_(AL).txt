
        MASS--Alvin (Al), passed away at home on December 10th after a long but valiant battle with Melanoma. He is survived by his wife Barbara, daughters Zannah and Rachel, son-in-law Aaron, niece Marian, grand-niece Talia, sister Madeline and a huge circle of friends and extended family.
