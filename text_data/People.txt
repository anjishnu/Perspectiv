
        Tom Allon, publisher at Manhattan Media, New York, was promoted to the new posts of president and chief executive; he will continue as editor in chief. Mr. Allon is being succeeded as publisher by Diane Straus Tucker, who was most recently the publisher at The Westchester County Times and The Fairfield County Times, based in New Milford, Conn., which are now owned by the Journal Register Company.  John Isaf, senior vice president at Arnold Public Relations, Boston, part of Arnold Worldwide, was named to lead a new business consulting service, Arnold Capital Insight. Bill Alberti, senior account executive at Arnold Public Relations,  was named planning manager at Arnold Capital. Arnold is part of the Arnold Worldwide Partners division of Havas Advertising.
