
        WERTHEIMER--Jane Celler, age 89, of New Rochelle, NY on December 12. Beloved wife of the late Sydney Wertheimer, loving mother of Sue Serphos, Harrison, NY and Jill Rifkin, Delmar, NY. Also survived by sons-in-law James Serphos and Richard Rifkin, grandchildren Jennifer and Melissa Rifkin and Jeffrey Kuskin, and greatgrandchildren Lauren and Janet Kuskin and Brayden Serphos. Daughter of the late Brooklyn Congressman Emanuel Celler and Stella Celler. Founder and President of Passkey Associates, a special events firm in Manhattan. Aficionado of opera and lifelong defender of the Democratic party. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to favorite charities. Funeral service at BallardDurand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY at Noon Monday, December 18th. Visiting will be at the home of Mr. and Mrs. James Serphos, 3 Ramapo Circle, Harrison on Wednesday, December 20th, 7-9 pm.

We send our sincere condolences to the entire family in their time of sorrow. Her many years of friendship, kindness, and enthusiastic spirit will be remembered.
