At 2 p.m., (212) 247-7800, carnegiehall.org; $8.

Tonight at 8, tomorrow and Sunday at 3 and 8 p.m., at the school's theater, 647 St. Nicholas Avenue, near 141st Street, Hamilton Heights, (866) 527-5376, smarttix.com; $18; $15 for students, 65+ and groups.

Combined tickets: $23; children, $16; family-level members, $18 and $12. At 1109 Fifth Avenue, at 92nd Street, (212) 423-3337, thejewishmuseum.org.
