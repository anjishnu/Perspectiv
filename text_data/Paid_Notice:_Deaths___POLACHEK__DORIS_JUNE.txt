
        POLACHEK--Doris June, age 83, died in her Manhattan apartment on December 4, 2006. A graduate of Cornell University, Doris spent her professional career in the financial services industry and was a retired Vice-President of Merrill Lynch. She is survived by her sister and brother-in-law, Elinor and Robert Bleyer of Sarasota, FL, as well as three nephews and two grand nephews. Plans for a Memorial are not finalized.
