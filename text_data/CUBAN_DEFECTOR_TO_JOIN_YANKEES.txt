
        The Yankees are expected to sign Juan Miranda, 23, a left-handed hitting first baseman-outfielder who defected from Cuba in 2004. Miranda told ESPN.com he agreed to a four-year, $2 million deal.
        According to the Official Cuban Baseball Guide, Miranda has a .301 career average. The Yankees do not expect Miranda to reach the major leagues in 2007.
