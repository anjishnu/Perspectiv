
        FAILLACE--Carolyn Louise of New Lebanon, NY passed on Friday, December 10, 2006 at the age of 53. She was a graduate of Pratt University in Fashion Design and studied Millinery at FIT in NYC, and created the First American Crafts Gallery in Soho in the 1970's. She belonged to the National Historic Register, and had a passion for gardening. She is survived by her mother Pauline, brothers Dennis and Robert, and by her beloved husband Alan Terry Twigg. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, December 16th at 2pm.
