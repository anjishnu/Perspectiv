
        To the Editor:
        Re ''Keep the E.P.A. Libraries Open,'' by Leslie Burger (Op-Ed, Dec. 8): Why would any sane government official think it is in the public interest to close Environmental Protection Agency libraries and thus limit access to information about the health of the soil, water and air in states and neighborhoods around this country?
        Mel Minthorn  Wilton, Conn., Dec. 8, 2006
