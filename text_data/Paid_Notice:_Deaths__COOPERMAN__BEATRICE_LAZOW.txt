
        COOPERMAN--Beatrice Lazow, 88, of Deerfield Beach, FL. Passed away December 6th, 2006. She is survived by: beloved husband Harvey; loving children Carol Halper (Steve), Dr. Elliot Cooperman (Robyn); grandchildren Lisa, Rick (Jennifer), Rachel, Hannah, Rebecca, Chris, Danielle; great-grandchildren Dylan, Eden, Summer; and all who knew and loved her. Services were held on Monday, December 11th, 2006 in Deerfield Beach, FL. Interment was at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont NY.
