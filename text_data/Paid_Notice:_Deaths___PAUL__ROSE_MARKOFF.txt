
        PAUL--Rose Markoff. December 14, age 92. Devoted wife of the late Samuel D. Paul; loving mother of Gerald (Sherri) and Kenneth (Sevara); proud grandmother of Sandy (Leslie), Amy (Jonathan) and Kathryn, and greatgrandmother of Emma. She loved her family, teaching and learning. Service at 12:45pm, December 17, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 St. Memorial contributions may be made to the United Negro College Fund.
