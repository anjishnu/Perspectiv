
        SHAFER--Raymond P., The Board of Directors and staff of the National Committee on U.S.-China Relations mourn the death of Governor Ray Shafer, our chairman between 1982-1992. Through his leadership, wise counsel, and good will, he made an enduring contribution to constructive U.S.-China relations and to our organization. We are forever grateful for his service, and extend our condolences to his wife Jane, son Phillip, and daughter Diane.
