
        GROBMAN--Hulda. Professor Emeritus of Education at St. Louis University Medical Center on December 10th, 2006 in Gainesville, FL. Dr. Grobman was a Professor of Education at New York University and Brooklyn College, a staff consultant for the Biological Sciences Curriculum Study, on the boards of the Florida Department of Children and Families, and the League of Women Voters, and had a weekly column in the Gainesville Sun. The daughter of the late Joseph Gross and Dora Abrahams, the sister of the late Herbert Gross, she is survived by her husband of 61 years, Dr. Arnold Grobman, her children, Marc Grobman and Beth Grobman, and her grandson David Burruss.
