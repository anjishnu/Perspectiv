
        Dara Elizabeth Schatt, the daughter of Patricia Shapiro Schatt and Lawrence Edward Schatt of Manhattan, is to be married this afternoon to Jason Phillip Rogoff, a son of Deborah S. Rogoff and Rabbi Chaim A. Rogoff of East Brunswick, N.J. The bridegroom's father is to officiate at the Hilton East Brunswick.
        The bride, 25, is a speech pathologist at Blythedale Children's Hospital in Valhalla, N.Y. She graduated from the University of Wisconsin and received a master's in speech from Emerson College.
        Her father retired as a deputy commissioner of the New York City Department of Environmental Protection in the Giuliani administration. Her mother is the vice president for operations at Paradigm Property Management and Parkchester Preservation Management, two firms in the Bronx that manage residential properties.
        The bridegroom, also 25, is a doctoral candidate in his third year at the Jewish Theological Seminary, where he is also an adjunct lecturer teaching courses in the Talmud and Jewish law. He graduated from Columbia.
        His mother is a learning-disabilities teacher consultant at the Quibbletown Middle School in Piscataway, N.J. His father is the rabbi of the East Brunswick Jewish Center.
