
        STODDARD--Carole L. died on December 13, 2006. Born August 18, 1935 in Brooklyn, NY. Graduate of Cornell University. Retired copyright editor for Dell Publishing. Continued on as volunteer school librarian and tutor. She is survived by daughter Judith, son-in-law Johnnial, stepson Mark, grandchildren Edward, Ahtiaana, Jazmine, Tamara, Christina, Nina, Helen, dog Daisy and cats Charlie and Zeek. She will be remembered for her kindness, generosity, keen wit and strong will. Memorial Service, December 21, 4pm, Grace Church, 802 Broadway.
