
        CHARNEY--David, husband of Louise, father of Esther, Steve and Ken, died December 12. He was 83. Dave was a renaissance man. A lieutenant in the Air Force in WWII, a gymnast, an art director, 7th degree black belt, author, flamenco guitarist, college professor and actor. There will be a memorial gathering from 5pm to 7pm held on Monday December 18 at the McGrath Funeral Home at 20 Cedar Street in Bronxville.
