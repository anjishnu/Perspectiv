
        HABER--Robert, D.D.S. Passed away December 18th, at the age of 82. A skilled dentist, and ace Navy pilot in World War II. Beloved husband to Carole Wright, devoted father to John, Ilene, and Alex, and loving brother to Stan. Adored by family and friends for his humor, charm, and intelligence. We will miss you forever. Services today, December 19th, 12:45pm at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave, NYC.
