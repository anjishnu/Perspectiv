
        Charles Jones, a gardener and photographer who worked in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, created stunning photographs of vegetables and flowers. Through Jan. 13, 29 of his gelatin silver prints, the largest exhibit of his work in this country, are on display at Howard Greenberg Gallery, 41 East 57th Street, (212) 334-0010; it is open Tuesday through Saturday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. If you find you have to own one, they cost from $7,000 to $20,000 each.
