
        A 57-year-old man was accused of selling crack last night while pushing a stroller carrying an 18-month-old girl, the police said. The man, Michael Lewis, was spotted dealing crack on Guy R. Brewer Boulevard in Springfield Gardens around 5 p.m., the police said. He is charged with selling drugs and endangering a minor. The Administration for Children's Services is expected to take custody of the girl, whose relationship to Mr. Lewis was unclear.
