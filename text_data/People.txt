
        Ron Lawner, vice chairman and global chief creative officer at Arnold Worldwide, Boston, part of the Arnold Worldwide Partners unit of Havas, is leaving, effective Dec. 31. Mr. Lawner, 58, has worked at Arnold and predecessor agencies since 1981 on accounts like Fidelity and Volkswagen. Mr. Lawner plans to work as a consultant and help MassProduction attract commercial production to the Boston market.
        Wieden & Kennedy named a separate management team for its flagship Portland, Ore., office as the worldwide managers of the agency -- John Jay, Dave Luhr and Dan Wieden -- ceded their Portland duties to focus globally. In Portland, Tom Blessington becomes managing director, while Jelly Helm and Steve Luker become co-creative directors.
