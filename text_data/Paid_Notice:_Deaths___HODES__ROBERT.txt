
        HODES--Robert. The Board of Trustees and Staff of Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts regret the passing of Robert D. Hodes, friend, neighbor and generous supporter. Bob Hodes served as a Trustee of Caramoor from 1985-2000, including terms as Treasurer and Vice Chairman. His appreciation of our mission was enthusiastic and his advice and counsel essential. We extend our sympathies to his wife Gerry and to his family. Judy Evnin, Chairman
        $(6$)HODES--Robert Daniel, passed away peacefully on December 18th, 2006. He was 79 years old and lived in Katonah, New York and Naples, Florida. Born in Brooklyn, NY, Bob attended Poly Prep and graduated cum laude from Princeton University in 1949. After graduation, Bob joined his father, Robert M. Hodes, at Hodes-Daniel Company, a leading supplier of direct mail advertising to the financial community. Bob succeeded his father in 1961 as CEO of the company. In 1976, Hodes-Daniel was acquired by Ogilvy & Mather Advertising. It was renamed Ogilvy & Mather Direct where Bob served as its first Chairman. Bob is survived by his wife of 53 years, Gerry Heller Hodes; his four children and their spouses: Debbie Hodes of Westport, CT, Jean and Jeffrey Shaw of New Canaan, CT, Susie and Richard O'Leary of Greenwich, CT, and Robert Jr. and Sheila Hodes, of St. Louis, MO; and his nine grandchildren. Contributions in his memory can be made to any of the following: 1948 Hodes Scholarship Fund at Princeton University, c/o Nancy Kalmikoff, Gift Records, P.O. Box 5357, Princeton, NJ 08543-5357; The Cornell Memory Disorders Program, c/o Tom Horton, Neurology, Weill Cornell Medical College, 1300 York Ave., New York, NY 10021; or Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts, The Rising Star Program, Box 816, Katonah, NY 10536. The family will be at home in Katonah at 180 Girdle Ridge Road on Tuesday, December 26 and Wednesday, December 27 from 4 to 6 p.m. A memorial service to celebrate Bob's life will be held on Sunday, January 7 at 2:30 p.m. at Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts, 149 Girdle Ridge Road, Katonah, NY 10536.
