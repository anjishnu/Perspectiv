
        BLACKER--Claire (nee Weiskopf) age 94, December 10, 2006. Predeceased by her beloved husband, Samuel. She is survived by Heather and Martin Abbe and Herbert and Sheila Blacker. Grandmother to David, Melissa, Judianne, Tracey, Jonathan and Monica. Claire was a vibrant, loving and devoted wife, mother, grandmother and aunt. She will be missed by all who were fortunate enough to know her. Funeral Services Tuesday, December 12, 2006, 11:30 am at Hellman Memorial Chapels, 15 State Street, Spring Valley, NY. Interment to follow at Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
