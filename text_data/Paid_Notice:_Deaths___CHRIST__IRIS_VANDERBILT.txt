
        CHRIST--Iris Vanderbilt, died on December 8, 2006. A fifth generation descendant of Commodore Cornelius Vanderbilt, Mrs. Christ was survived by her mother, Consuelo Vanderbilt Earl, her sister, Virginia S. Burke, her brother Earl E.T. Smith, Jr., her son Peter Van Ingen, her daughters, Serena Van Ingen McCallum and Helen Paine Englehardt, her husband, Donald C. Christ, and nine grandchildren. Memorial and burial private. Contributions to Memorial Sloan Kettering Hospital, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021, would be appreciated.
