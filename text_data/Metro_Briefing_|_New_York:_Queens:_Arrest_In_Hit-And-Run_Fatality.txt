
        A Queens mechanic was arrested yesterday and charged in the hit-and-run death of a 12-year-old boy on Saturday, the police said. The mechanic, Juan Franjul, 46, of Richmond Hill, was charged with manslaughter in the death of Jacob Colon of Queens. Mr. Franjul is charged with driving a black Mercedes that belonged to a customer when, the police say, he hit the boy at the corner of 108th Street and Atlantic Avenue on the border of Richmond Hill and Ozone Park. The owner of the Mercedes helped the police identify Mr. Franjul, who is also being charged with speeding, failure to stop at a red light, leaving the scene of a death and leaving the scene of property damage.
