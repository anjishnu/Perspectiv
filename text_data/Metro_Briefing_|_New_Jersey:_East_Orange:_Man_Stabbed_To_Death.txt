
        A 24-year-old man was fatally stabbed early yesterday while attending a party at St. John's Lodge on Central Avenue, the authorities said. The man, Charles Mathis of Newark, was stabbed in the chest about 3:25 a.m. and was discovered in a hallway near the club's dance floor, said Paul M. Loriquet, a spokesman for the Essex County prosecutor's office.
