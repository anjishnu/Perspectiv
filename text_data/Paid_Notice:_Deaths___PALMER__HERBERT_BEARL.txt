
        PALMER--Herbert Bearl, 91, Art innovator, Gallerist, and prominent figure in the Los Angeles art world. Died peacefully on December 12th, 2006. His wife Lillian, his son and daughter-in-law, William and Anne Thorson Palmer, and daughter Meredith Palmer, will deeply miss his inspiration and love.
