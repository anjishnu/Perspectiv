
        GLASER--Agathe Maier, 78. Devoted wife of the late Rabbi Joseph B. Glaser. Beloved mother of Simeon and Barbara Glaser, Meyer Glaser and Katherine Kohrman, Sara Glaser, and Jack Glaser and Elissa Epel, grandmother of Benjamin, Louis, Hannah, Eliana, Lila, Yossi and Danny, sister of Louis Maier. Vivacious, sun-shiny storyteller, art teacher, gardner, rebitzen extraordinaire-she enchanted little children and adults alike with the warmth of Jewish tradition. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Sigmund and Clara Maier Education Fund for Innercity Youth, c/o Temple Israel, 2324 Emerson Avenue South, Minneapolis, MN 55405.
