
        ROSENTHAL--Nancy, of Great Neck died December 3rd, 2006 at Calvary Hospital after a three year bout with colon cancer. She was 62 and a retired English teacher in NYC Jr. High Schools. Born in Brooklyn and a graduate of Erasmus Hall HS., Brooklyn College (Phi Beta Kappa) and Boston University, she is survived by her husband, Paul Rosendahl; a brother Robert of Manhattan; a sister Denise Glick and her stepmother, Pearl, both of Asheville, NC. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions in Nancy's memory may be made to Calvary hospital, 1740 Eastchester Rd., Bronx NY, 10461.
