
        ZUCKERMAN--Leonard. A pioneer in the instant coffee industry died on December 14th, 2006. He was the beloved husband of Anna, father of Marcia, Charles and Carol, father-in-law of Jonathan Feuer and Naomi Zuckerman, grandfather of Lauren, Ethan, Molly, Daniel and Julia, and brother of Dossy Freifeld. Donations may be made to the Stem Cell Research Foundation.
