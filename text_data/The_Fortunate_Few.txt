
        To the Editor:
        Re ''The Man Museums Love to Hate'' by Stephanie Strom [Dec. 10]:
        I am a left-wing liberal and I find myself wholly in agreement with Senator Charles E. Grassley, a Republican. It is outrageous that billionaires have found another way to amass untold riches by being allowed to save on taxes for so-called partial gifts of art.
        Anita J. Seyler  Allenhurst, N.J.
