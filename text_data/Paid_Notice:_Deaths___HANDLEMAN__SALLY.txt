
        HANDLEMAN--Sally. The Officers, Board of Trustees and staff of American Friends of Magen David Adom, ARMDI mourn the passing of Sally Handleman, widow of Joseph Handleman, who died on December 8, 2006, she was 101 years of age. She and her husband, Joseph, who predeceased her, were the primary benefactors of Magen David Adom Blood Services Center in Ramat Gan, Israel. We extend our sincerest condolences to the entire Handleman family. May her memory be a blessing.
