
        STONE--Allan Barry, 74, of Purchase, NY died peacefully in his sleep on December 15, 2006. A beloved husband, father, grandfather, friend, collector and art dealer, his extraordinary joie de vivre, brilliance, humor, generosity and acumen were an inspiration to all who knew him. He will be greatly missed. He is survived by his wife Clare Stone, his daughters and sons-in-law Allison and Vincent Stabile, Jeremy Stone and Paul Matz, Claudia Stone and Goran Sare, Heather Stone, Jessie Stone and Olympia (Posy) Stone and Sims Preston his Brother Richard Stone, his sister Marilyn Siegel, and his grandchildren Christopher Stabile, Joshua Stabile, Lucy Matz, Hudson Matz, Nicholas Sare, Emma Sare, Colby Preston, Theo Preston. A celebration of his life will be held January, 2007, location to be determined. In lieu of flowers, donations to: Soft Power Health to Allan Stone Gallery, 113 East 90th Street, New York, NY 10128. In the meantime have a few scoops of ice cream in his honor.
