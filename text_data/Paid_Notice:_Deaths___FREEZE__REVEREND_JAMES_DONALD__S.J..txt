
        FREEZE--Reverend James Donald, S.J., died on December 10th, 2006 in Merion Station, PA. Born in 1932 in Baltimore, MD. Rev. Freeze was executive vice president for academic affairs and provost at Georgetown University from 1979-1991. A funeral Mass for Rev. Freeze will be celebrated Saturday, December 16th, 2006, at 11:00 a.m. at the Alumni Memorial Chapel at Loyola College in Maryland.
