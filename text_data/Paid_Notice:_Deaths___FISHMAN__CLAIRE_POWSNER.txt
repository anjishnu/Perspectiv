
        FISHMAN--Claire Powsner. Died December 17, 2006 at age 79. Beloved wife of Fred N. Fishman, mother of Robert and Nancy, grandmother of Evan and Tyler. She was a loving and courageous woman, a caring friend and a devoted teacher. Services Thursday December 21 at 11:30am at ''The Riverside'', Amsterdam Ave. at 76th St. Visiting hours 48pm Wednesday December 20 at ''The Riverside''. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Harvard University Faculty of Arts and Sciences, 124 Mt. Auburn St., Cambridge MA 02138 for the Claire Powsner Fishman Scholarship Fund (preference to immigrant and first - generation undergra duates).
